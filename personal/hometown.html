Brandon's Hometown Places


Photo by Julian Myles

Malibu, California
This is where I was born and raised. Many of television shows and movies are filmed here and it is the home of many movie stars. I wouldn't know if recognized any at the time growing up, but I enjoyed going to the beach a lot and surfing. Though, it isn't a really fun place to grow up since there isn't much to do if you're a kid, except the beach or hiking. Still, it was nice going to bed to the sound of the waves each night, so that was pleasant.



Photo by Matthew Lejune

Santa Monica, California
Since Malibu was sort of boring as a teenager, my friends and I would spend a lot of time in Santa Monica. There's the Third Street Promenade with all its shopping, Montana Ave with restaurants and boutiques, and the Pier that has a rollercoaster and carnival games. It was usually a good day to go surfing when the sun was up and relaxing on the pier at night.



Photo by Juliana Malta

Venice, California
This by far is one of my favorite places on Earth. It is just north of Santa Monica and I would spend a lot of time here. Their infamous boardwalk has a lot of shops and oddities while Abbot & Kinney has many cafes and bars. The culture is very much about art and music with a very relaxed, beach vibe. I always felt comfortable here, more so than even Malibu.
